About Me

My name is Lance Schmittling and this is the online visual archive of my work. I consider myself fortunate, because my job entails doing what I love. I call myself a designer as it's a nice label for the print design / web design / illustration / motion design / branding work that I do.

I have an extensive background with design. Starting at a young age, I collected action figures, comic books (X-Men & Spider-Man), and woke up early every Saturday for cartoons. My design education taught me how to effectively communicate through visual imagery. As a scholar of design, I am always teaching myself new things and firmly believe that practice makes perfect.

I have always been interested in learning web development, and recently took the necessary steps to begin this journey. I want to be a well-rounded designer, and this is a big step to becoming just that.
